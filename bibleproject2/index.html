Preparation And Skill

David was skilled with a sling, which he had practiced during his time as a shepherd. Though others may have dismissed his experience, David used his skill effectively.

Humility and Confidence

David's confidence was with his relationship with God, he always tried to remained humble. He didn't boast in his own strength or seek personal glory instead, he gave credit to God for the victory.
